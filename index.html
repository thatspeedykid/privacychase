Philosophy

How we think
about software.

01

Your data, your device

Nothing leaves your device without you actively doing it. No background sync. No telemetry. No analytics. Zero network requests — ever.

02

No accounts, ever

We'll never ask you to sign up for anything. No email. No password. No profile. If you want to move your data, you copy a single line of text and paste it somewhere else.

03

Free, open source

Every line of code is public on GitHub. MIT licensed. No freemium trap. No premium tier. Build it yourself, fork it, verify nothing sketchy is happening.

About

One developer.
No compromises.

PrivacyChase is an indie software studio run by a single developer. No team, no investors, no board to answer to — which means no pressure to monetize your attention or your data.

The apps are built in Flutter, target every major platform, and share a design philosophy: your personal data belongs to you and shouldn't require trusting a startup to stay in business.

All development happens in the open on GitHub. Contributions, feedback, and bug reports are welcome.
