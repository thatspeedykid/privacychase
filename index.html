Flowe

The file-first budgeting app.

Most budget apps want your email, your bank login, and a monthly fee. Flowe doesn't. Your data lives in a single file on your device. Back it up however you want — no cloud sync, no subscription, no account to forget the password to.

  • Monthly budget with income & expense tracking
  • Debt snowball simulator with payoff dates
  • Net worth snapshots with delta tracking
  • Event budgets & split calculator
  • Export to CSV or PDF — zero lock-in
  • One-tap copy backup — paste it anywhere to restore

Auris

Privacy-first audio enhancement.

Your headphones, everywhere. Auris makes your headphones sound as good on Windows as they do on the manufacturer's app — with zero telemetry, zero cloud, and zero compromise. Auto-detects your device and applies correction curves from a 1,400+ headphone database.

  • Headphone profiles — AutoEQ database, 1,400+ devices
  • Per-app audio profiles — Spotify, Discord, games each get their own EQ
  • On-device noise suppression via DeepFilterNet 3 — no cloud
  • AI scene detection — music, calls, gaming — auto-switches preset
  • Built-in privacy audit log — should always be empty
  • Zero network calls during normal operation
Windows 10/11 macOS (planned) Linux (planned)

Philosophy

How we think
about software.

01

Your data, your device

Nothing leaves your device without you actively doing it. No background sync. No telemetry. No analytics. Zero network requests — ever.

02

No accounts, ever

We'll never ask you to sign up for anything. No email. No password. No profile. If you want to move your data, you copy a single line of text and paste it somewhere else.

03

Free, open source

Every line of code is public on GitHub. MIT licensed. No freemium trap. No premium tier. Build it yourself, fork it, verify nothing sketchy is happening.

About

One developer.
No compromises.

PrivacyChase is an indie software studio run by a single developer. No team, no investors, no board to answer to — which means no pressure to monetize your attention or your data.

The apps are built in Flutter, target every major platform, and share a design philosophy: your personal data belongs to you and shouldn't require trusting a startup to stay in business.

All development happens in the open on GitHub. Contributions, feedback, and bug reports are welcome.
