Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — applies to Flowe and Fuelle

The short version

PrivacyChase apps collect zero personal data. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. Everything you enter stays on your device. We never see it. We never could.

Who we are

PrivacyChase is an independent, single-developer software studio. We make Flowe (personal finance) and Fuelle (meal planning & nutrition tracking). Our apps are available on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS.

Contact: networkchasemedia@gmail.com

What data we collect

None. Our apps do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or account credentials are ever requested or stored.
  • No financial data from Flowe leaves your device.
  • No nutrition or meal data from Fuelle leaves your device.
  • No usage statistics, crash reports, or analytics are sent anywhere.
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data are collected.
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party tracking tools are included in any app.

How your data is stored

All data is stored locally in a single file on your own device. The exact location depends on your platform:

  • Windows: %APPDATA%\[appname]\data.json
  • Linux: ~/.local/share/[appname]/data.json
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/[appname]/data.json
  • iOS / Android: Private app storage — accessible via the in-app backup feature

You are in full control of this file. You can back it up, move it, delete it, or share it however you like. We have no access to it.

The backup & restore feature

Both apps include a backup feature that encodes your data into a compact text string (using gzip + base64). This string is copied to your clipboard only when you explicitly tap "Copy Backup." It is never sent to any server. You can paste it wherever you choose — that is entirely under your control.

When you use "Paste & Restore," the app reads from your clipboard. Again, nothing is transmitted anywhere.

Network access

Flowe makes zero network requests. It is fully offline.

Fuelle makes a single type of network request: searching the USDA FoodData Central database for food items when you perform a food search. This request goes directly to the USDA's public API. No personal data is included in this request — only the search term you type. No other network requests are made.

Children's privacy

Our apps do not collect any information from anyone, including children. We have no mechanism to do so. Our apps comply with COPPA and equivalent regulations by design — by collecting nothing at all.

Third-party services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social login services. The only third-party service touched is the USDA FoodData Central API by Fuelle, as described above.

This website

This website (privacychase.com) is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard web server data (IP addresses, browser user agents) as part of their hosting service. We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies on this website. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for information on their data practices.

Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. Your data exists only on your own device, and you have full control over it at all times.

If you are in the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA), or another jurisdiction with privacy rights, those rights are effectively already fulfilled by our architecture: we simply do not have your data.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices ever change (for example, if a future version of an app adds optional cloud sync), we will update this policy and note the date at the top. We will never collect data without clearly disclosing it here first.
