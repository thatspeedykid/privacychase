What the Apps do

Flowe is a personal finance and budgeting tool. It helps you track income, expenses, debts, net worth, and event budgets. All data is stored locally on your device.

Fuelle is a meal planning and nutrition tracking tool. It lets you log meals and track macros using the USDA FoodData Central database. All data is stored locally on your device, except for food search queries sent to the USDA's public API.

Auris is a privacy-first audio enhancement application for Windows (with macOS and Linux planned). It applies headphone correction profiles, per-app EQ, and on-device noise suppression. All processing is local. An optional, user-initiated update check may contact the GitHub Releases API.
